Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Textora respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your information.

Information We Process

Textora processes text that users explicitly select and submit for AI-powered writing assistance, including grammar correction, text improvement, rewriting, translation, and other writing-related features.

How Information Is Used

Submitted text is used solely to provide requested functionality and generate responses.

Data Collection

Textora does not collect personal information such as names, addresses, phone numbers, or payment information.

Text submitted for processing may be transmitted to third-party AI providers solely for the purpose of generating requested results.

Data Sharing

Textora does not sell, rent, or share personal information with advertisers or data brokers.

User Control

Users control when text is submitted for processing. Permissions granted through macOS may be modified or revoked at any time through system settings.

Security

Reasonable technical and organizational measures are used to protect transmitted information.
